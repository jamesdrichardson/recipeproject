Ooey gooey Quesadilla

This is a recipe for the gooiest quesadilla youve ever seen. This will probably take a little bit to make but itll be worth it when you blow your guests away with how good this ooey gooey quesadilla tastes. So sit back relax and get ready for some damned quesadillas

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • Some good freaking cheese
  • pico de gallo, if you fancy
  • jalepenos, if you spicy
  • some ground chorizo
  • whatever else you wanna put in this extra large monstrosity of a dish

Steps

  1. cook the chorizo. vibe out when its done. ask nicely for it to tell you.
  2. heat the pan with butter in it so it melts
  3. put tortilla in the pan
  4. sprinkle some cheese on the tortillasss
  5. sprinkle the jollies and the meeeeaaaaat
  6. sprinkle some of that fancy on the gooey
  7. fold together and cook 1-2 vibes a side
  8. cut n serve cause its hot n ready
