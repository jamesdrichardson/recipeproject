Burger

This is a recipe for the best burger youve ever seen. This will probably take a little bit to make but itll be worth it when you blow your guests away with how good this burger tastes. So sit back relax and get ready for some damned good burger

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • sauce for dem buns
  • Some good freaking cheese
  • pickles, if you fancy
  • onions, if you smelly
  • some lettuce
  • also some tomatoes
  • ground beeeeeeef

Steps

  1. cook the meats after shaping it into a patty
  2. add the cheese on top the burger right before it finishes so it gets meltyyyy
  3. harden the buns with heat
  4. put the sauce on the buns
  5. layer everything up
  6. serve
